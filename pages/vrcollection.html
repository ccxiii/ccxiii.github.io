LEARN CHINESE WITH AR

Other Team Members

Qing Guo, Mu He, Yu Qin, Yue Wang, Yifan Zhao

In this team project, we aims at helping beginners in Chinese learning to quickly grasp daily communication through immersive learning experience in VR.

Low Fidelity Prototype

To better identify the idea we were about to continue in VR prototype, we created a low fidelity prototype to simulate the process.

When user's eyesight "hovers" the object, popup of object's information will appear

We decide to use a "3D map" to allow users choose which scene they want to "go into"

We quickly brainstormed for how the scene would be with paper storyboard and play-doh.

Interactive Prototype

For this VR prototpye, I quickly sketched a 360 images for scene building, built on A-Frame & glitch. Free to check it here or visit this link to have a better experience

While this prototype only supports 2 interactions: looking around and listening to background music. It roughly expresses out idea of what a scene would be.

INSPIRATION COLLECTOR

Other Team Members

Qing Guo, Mu He, Yu Qin, Yue Wang, Yifan Zhao

This is an AR project called inspiration collector. The main feature of it includes: scan and recognize object, store them in the object in 3D, collect color features, and share asset with others.
